During this: SENATOR (who is mildly surprised and faintly embarrassed) Err...the Supreme Court story, I was telling you - Justice Black. HUNSECKER (nodding) Yes, the Justice, that's right. But I think you had it in the column. SIDNEY (smoothly, casually) Last July, the lead item... Sidney's interjection is quietly well-mannered. Hunsecker totally ignores it. The other members of the party are a little astonished at the interplay. The girl, in particular, is fascinated; she clearly admires Sidney's looks. The Senator, noting this, glances at Sidney, accepting the point: SENATOR (laughing) And I believe that's precisely where I read it, too.
