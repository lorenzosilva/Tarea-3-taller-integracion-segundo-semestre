JUDGE And as for you, barber, it is all too clear what company you keep. Service them well and hold their custom -- for you'll have none of mine. He strides out. Todd stands, frozen. ANTHONY Mr. Todd -- you have to help me -- I've talked to Johanna and--! Todd suddenly turns on him with a ferocious ROAR: TODD OUT! OUT, I SAY! Utterly stunned at his friend's ferocity, Anthony backs away, leaves the shop. Music begins, very agitated, as Todd stands motionless. In shock. His mind cracking apart. Mrs. Lovett hurries in: MRS. LOVETT All this shouting and running about, what's happened--? TODD I had him -- and then-- MRS.
