He stops, lost, trying to get his bearings, studying his map. Then an unusual sound emerges through the normal cosmopolitan bustle. It is the sound of a woman humming. He looks up to see... JOHANNA, a 16-year-old girl with golden hair, beautiful and hauntingly sad. She sits at her window above, behind bars, humming to herself as she does needlepoint. Anthony watches her, absolutely mesmerized. Johanna notices a Bird Seller passing. He carries a long, wooden pole with little bird cages attached. JOHANNA Green finch and linnet bird, Nightingale, blackbird, How is it you sing? How can you jubilate, Sitting in cages, Never taking wing?
