The music becomes ferocious as Todd's wrenching insanity, always close to the surface, finally explodes: TODD There's a hole in the world Like a great black pit And it's filled with people Who are filled with shit And the vermin of the world Inhabit it-- But not for long! He suddenly looks to Mrs. Lovett -- she starts back -- alarmed by the pure madness in his eyes-- TODD They all deserve to die!
