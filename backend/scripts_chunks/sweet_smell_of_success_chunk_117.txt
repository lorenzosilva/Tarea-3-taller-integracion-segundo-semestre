PALACE THEATRE - DAY Sidney comes down 47th Street from Broadway, making for the stage door entrance of the Palace theatre. He walks confidently into the alleyway, paying no attention to the old doorman gossiping with the shoeshine boy at the chairs next to the entrance. The doorman turns, protesting: DOORMAN (calling out) Hey! LAP DISSOLVE TO: Sidney, without halting, looks back towards the Doorman, addressing him with the patronizing manner of a superior.
