Three Hummers sit in the middle of the floor, surrounded by broken glass. Between them rests a bright red Ferrari F50 (Gabriel's). All but one of the front windows of the bank, the one with the door in it, has been welded over with 3/4 inch plate steel. Over two dozen hostages lie face down on the floor, arms cable-tied behind their backs. Something has been duct- taped around their chests and each is wearing what appears to be a dog collar.
