TOBY My heart bleeds for you havin' to go up and down all these stairs! MRS. LOVETT Well, that'll be your job now. TOBY Yes, ma'am! She arrives at the heavy iron door to the bakehouse. We can hear the seismic rumble of the bake oven within. She unbolts the door and ushers Toby in. And we finally enter... 103 INT. THE BAKEHOUSE -- EVENING 103 A macabre vision of Hell. The roof hangs low in this subterranean chamber. The grisly tools of her trade are scattered about the place: a large, stained chopping block; a meat grinder; buckets of questionable viscous liquid; cleavers and bone saws and meat hooks; wet sewer grates for the blood.
