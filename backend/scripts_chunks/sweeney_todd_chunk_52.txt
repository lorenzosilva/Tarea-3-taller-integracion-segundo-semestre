GENTLEMAN Sweeney pondered and Sweeney planned. Like a perfect machine 'e planned... The Banker moves in next to the Gentleman... BANKER Barbing the hook, baiting the trap, Setting it out for the Beadle to snap... The General joins them... GENERAL Slyly courted 'im, Sweeney did, Set a sort of a scene 'e did... GENTLEMAN, BANKER AND GENERAL Laying the trail, showing the traces, Letting it lead to higher places... Sweeney... The last word echoes ... And then they are gone ... disappearing from Todd's mind ... swallowed up by the crowd of pedestrians...
