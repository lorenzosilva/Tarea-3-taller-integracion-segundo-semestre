The music comes to a rousing conclusion as Mrs. Lovett stands at the door to her shop. Triumphant. 57 INT. BARBER SHOP -- NIGHT 57 Todd is alone. He sits in the barber chair, smoking a pipe. He is holding an old Daguerreotype; creased, stained and bleached-out. The image shows his wife, Lucy, smiling and holding Baby Johanna.
