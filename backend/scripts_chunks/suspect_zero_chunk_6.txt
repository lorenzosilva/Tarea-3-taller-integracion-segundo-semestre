The farther he gets away from that Diner, the happier he'll be. ...until a strange SOUND gets his attention: it's WIND, as if whistling through a ghost town. Hollow, varied - building then falling off again. Wind. Trouble is, the windows in this Buick are up. Yet there it is again: a thin, hollow GHOST-TOWN WIND, whistling over his shoulder. He's heard it before - in every Western he ever watched as a kid. But this wind is coming from his back-seat. The blood drains right out of Speck's face.
